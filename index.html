#1 Board Game Trend In Algeria

AZREB CHOOF

AzreB ChooF! is a speed and observation game
for the whole family. The aim of the game?
Each two cards have one symbol in common. Be the first to find and name it to win the card.

GAME RULES

Before playing

If you have never played or are playing with people who have never played, take two cards at random and place them face up on the table in the center of the players. Search the identical symbol between the two cards (same shape, only the size may be different). The first player to find it, names it and takes two new cards that he places on the table. Repeat the operation until all players have understood that there is always one and only one identical symbol between two cards

Points system for tournaments

Start with “The Lighthouse”, the loser chooses the next mini-game. The lighthouse: +1 point per card recovered / +5 points for the player who collected the most cards. The pit: +10 points for the first player to get rid of all his cards / -20 points for the last one. The Crumpled Old Man: +20 points for the player who recovered the fewest cards / + 10 points for the second. The hot cake: -5 points per round lost The conquistador: +1 point per card recovered. The debtor: -1 point per remaining card

In case of doubt

The player who names the symbol first wins! If the players spoke at the same time, it is the one who took or placed his card is the first to win.

Ex-aequo

At the end of a game, the tied players have a duel (or a round of hot cake if there are more than two) They each draw a card and turn it over at the same time, the first to find the identical symbol and names it, wins the duel.

Game over

The player who has won the most mini-games wins the game., For competition enthusiasts, a points system is proposed at the end of the rules.

Game goal

Whatever the game, you always have to be the quickest to spot the identical symbol between two cards, name it out loud, then take the card or place it according to the rules of the mini-game you are playing.
